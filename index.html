  I Love My City


As it turns out, photography can be quite the practice in optimism. Before I owned a camera, I would seldom find any beauty in the mundane, but now, with a camera in hand, that has changed entirely. I try to think of how I would photograph something that has been photographed many times before. Is there really a finite set of images that can come from any one thing? I don't believe so. For that reason, while many of my peers jump at the opportunity to leave the area , not me - I love my city!

In my circle of friends, "I Love My City" has become somewhat of a catch phrase. the book I plan to publish with that title will highlight some of the best discoveries I have made and documented in this town.
